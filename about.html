About Us

We are all final year students at Plymouth University studying varying digital design courses. We started work on this project in October 2020. This is a topic we all feel very strongly about. No animal deserves to go hungry, and no owner deserves to struggle feeding a family member who offers so much unconditional love. We hope Animal Fayre can offer people some hope during a period of such turmoil. Because we truly believe all pets deserve to be treated fairly. So whether you're here to receive or give we thank you for helping animals in need.
