About

Welcome to Animal Fayre! A site devoted to all animals and their owners. We hope we can help families who are struggling to feed the animals they love by giving them a place to come for assistance, and also give a place for people who wish to help others a place to advertise any food they may have that they don't need.

The coronavirus pandemic has been extremely financially difficult on families all around the country. This financial difficulty will for many affect people's ability to buy food for their little furry (or scaley) friend. With Animal Fayre we hope we can give people who are struggling to feed their pets a place to come and get food from people who are willing to share, because all animals deserve to be treated fairly.

When making Animal Fayre it was important for us to think about all animals, not just dogs, cats, rabbits and other fluffy varieties. So we also welcome donations for reptiles, birds, fish and any other type of animal. When making the donation just select the category of animal that best suits your pet and if you're not sure just choose exotic.
